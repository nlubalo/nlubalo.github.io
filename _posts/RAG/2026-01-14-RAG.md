<p>
How do you make large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4 smarter and more reliable for specific tasks it wasn't exactly trained on? Like Domain specific tasks about your company or industry? One powerful technique is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). RAG combines the strengths of LLMs with external knowledge sources to improve response accuracy and relevance.
</p>

<h2>What is RAG?</h2>
<p>
RAG is like giving your LLM a supercharged memory. Instead of relying solely on its pre-trained knowledge, RAG allows the model to retrieve relevant information from a large database or document collection during the generation process. This way, the model can provide answers based on up-to-date and context-specific data.
</p>
<p>
Its like students taking open book exams. They can look up information in their textbooks to answer questions more accurately instead of relying solely on what they memorized which may be outdated or incomplete.
</p>
